Increase or decrease font size in Word using keyboard shortcut

Updated: 05/13/2019 by Computer Hope

To increase or decrease the font size of text in Microsoft Word, follow these steps.

  1. Highlight the text that you want to make bigger or smaller.
  2. To increase the font size, press Ctrl+]. (Hold the Ctrl key, and press Right Bracket.)
  3. Or, to decrease the font size, press Ctrl+[.
