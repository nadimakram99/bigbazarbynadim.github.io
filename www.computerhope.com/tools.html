Computer Hope tools

Updated: 05/13/2019 by Computer Hope

Below is a listing of all the Computer Hope online tools we have available to the public.

Tip

Many of these tools are also integrated into our search found at the top and bottom of all our pages.

System Information

Our System Information Tool helps display all detectable system information capable of being found through your browser.

Windows processes and files

Our Windows processes and files tool helps give quick overviews of thousands of different windows processes and files.

Tip

This tool is integrated with our search and can be accessed by typing the name of a process or file in the search.

Mr. Hope

Our Mr. Hope tool helps you troubleshoot computer problems that are difficult to explain by going through a series of steps to help identify the issue.

Computer history

Our computer history displays the past computer events of every day of the month.

Tip

This tool is integrated with our search and can be accessed by typing a year into the search.

How many

Our How Many Tool helps get the differences between measurements (e.g., how many bits in a byte).

Tip

This tool is integrated with our search and can be accessed by asking a "how many" question.

Convert

Our Text Conversion Tool allows anyone to enter any text they want into a browser and have that text converted into many different forms of text.

HTML color codes

Our HTML Color Code Tool helps you get a complete overview of all HTML color codes and a listing of related colors.

Tip

This tool is integrated with our search and can be accessed by entering any color code.

Text Tool

Our Text Tool is capable of handling search and replaces and other conversions of any text.

Q&A

Our search is also integrated with a tool to help answer hundreds of thousands of computer-related questions. You can access this tool by asking a question (e.g., how to copy a file, what is copy, etc.) in our search boxes found at the top and bottom of all our pages.
